Dipayan Dey is a decision scientist and quantitative AI leader currently serving as a Vice President of AI/ML within BNY's Risk & Compliance division. Bridging statistical theory, causal inference, and decision intelligence, he leads the engineering of prescriptive modeling frameworks, dynamic risk threshold optimizations (MDPs), and technical AI governance under uncertainty. A graduate of the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Kanpur (M.Sc. in Statistics), he previously validated high-stakes algorithmic choices as an Associate at Goldman Sachs within Model Risk Management—spearheading causal validations for trade surveillance engines, designing the multi-agent "LLM Council" calibration gateway, and auditing Matryoshka embedding dynamics. Prior to Goldman, he engineered benchmarking frameworks for fuzzy matching and transaction monitoring models as an Associate at Morgan Stanley. He is passionate about probability theory, experiment design, and optimizing constraint-bound business decisions under uncertainty.
